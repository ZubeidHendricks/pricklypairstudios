Troubleshooting

The app is running slowly or crashing +

Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest app version
4. Clear app cache (Android) or reinstall (iOS)
5. Ensure you have enough storage space

If issues persist, contact support with your device model and iOS/Android version.

My data isn't syncing across devices +

To ensure data syncs properly:
1. Check that you're logged into the same account on both devices
2. Ensure both devices have internet connection
3. Go to Settings â†’ Sync â†’ Sync Now
4. Allow a few minutes for sync to complete
5. Try logging out and back in if sync still fails

Data is automatically backed up when you're connected to WiFi.
