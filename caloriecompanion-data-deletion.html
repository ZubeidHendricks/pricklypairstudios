Data Deletion Request

Request the permanent deletion of your CalorieCompanion account and all associated data.

⚠️

Important: This Action Cannot Be Undone

Once you request data deletion, your CalorieCompanion account and all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. This includes:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • All meal logs and nutrition data
  • Weight history and goal tracking
  • Food photos and notes
  • Progress reports and analytics
  • Subscription information (billing handled by app stores)

Option 1: Delete Through App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the CalorieCompanion app:

  1. Open CalorieCompanion on your device
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Enter your password to confirm your identity
  5. Review what will be deleted
  6. Tap "Permanently Delete Account"
  7. Confirm your decision in the final prompt

Note: This process is immediate and secure. You'll receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Option 2: Email Request

If you can't access the app, you can request deletion via email:

Send email to:

pricklypairstudiosza@gmail.com

Include in your email:

  • Subject: "CalorieCompanion Data Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your CalorieCompanion username (if different)
  • Reason for deletion (optional but helpful)
  • Confirmation: "I request permanent deletion of my account and all data"

Processing Time: Email requests are processed within 48 hours. You'll receive confirmation once deletion is initiated.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and profile information
  • Account credentials and login history
  • Device identifiers linked to your account
  • Communication preferences and settings

Health & Fitness Data

  • All meal logs and food entries
  • Weight history and body measurements
  • Fitness goals and progress tracking
  • Calorie intake and expenditure records

App Content

  • Food photos and meal images
  • Personal notes and comments
  • Custom food entries you created
  • App preferences and customizations

Analytics & Reports

  • Progress reports and insights
  • Usage statistics and app interactions
  • Exported data files and backups
  • Sync data across devices

What's NOT Deleted

Some information may be retained for legal, security, or operational purposes:

  • Payment Records: Handled by Apple/Google - contact them directly
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated data with no personal identifiers (retained up to 90 days)
  • Legal Records: If required by law or ongoing legal proceedings
  • Security Logs: Fraud prevention data (automatically expires after 90 days)
  • Support Communications: Previous support emails (unless specifically requested for deletion)

Before You Delete - Consider These Alternatives

Export Your Data First

Download your health data before deletion. Go to Settings → Account → Export Data to save your progress history.

Temporary Deactivation

Consider logging out instead of deleting. Your data will remain safe if you want to return later.

Privacy Settings

Adjust privacy settings instead. You can limit data collection and disable analytics in Settings → Privacy.

Contact Support

Having issues? Our support team can help resolve problems without needing to delete your account.

Ready to Delete?

If you're certain you want to proceed with deleting your CalorieCompanion account and all data, choose one of the methods above. Remember, this action cannot be undone.
