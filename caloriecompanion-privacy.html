CalorieCompanion Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025
Last Updated: January 2025

CalorieCompanion ("we," "us," or "our") is a health and fitness mobile application developed by Prickly Pair Studios. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application on iOS and Android devices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health and Fitness Data

  • Nutritional Information: Daily calorie intake, meals, food items, and nutritional values
  • Physical Data: Weight, height, age, gender, body measurements, and fitness goals
  • Activity Data: Exercise routines, calories burned, and activity levels
  • Dietary Preferences: Allergies, dietary restrictions, and food preferences
  • Progress Tracking: Weight history, calorie trends, and goal achievements

1.2 Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Username and password (encrypted)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Date of birth (for calorie calculation accuracy)

1.3 Device and Usage Information

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (for app functionality, not advertising)
  • App usage statistics and interaction data
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Time zone and language preferences

1.4 Optional Permissions

With your explicit consent, we may access:

  • Camera: To scan barcodes and take food photos for meal logging
  • Photo Library: To upload food images for your meal diary
  • Push Notifications: To send reminders for meal logging and goal updates
  • Health App Integration: To sync with Apple Health or Google Fit (if enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Calculate and track your daily calorie intake and expenditure
  • Provide personalized nutrition recommendations based on your goals
  • Generate progress reports and insights about your health journey
  • Store and display your meal history and favorite foods
  • Send reminders to log meals and stay on track with your goals
  • Sync data across your devices when logged into your account

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior and feedback
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Local Storage: Your recent meals and offline data are stored encrypted on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Account data and sync information are stored on secure servers
  • Backup: Optional cloud backup through your iCloud or Google account (if enabled)

3.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive health data
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmissions
  • Password protection with bcrypt hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Automatic logout after extended inactivity
  • No storage of payment information on our servers

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal or health data to third parties
  • Share your health information with advertisers
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share your information with insurance companies

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific cases:

  • Service Providers: Essential services like cloud hosting (AWS/Google Cloud) with strict data protection agreements
  • Analytics: Anonymized data with Firebase Analytics to improve app performance
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or valid legal process
  • Your Consent: With your explicit permission for specific features
  • Food Database: Non-personal queries to nutrition databases for food information

5. Your Rights and Controls

5.1 You Can Always:

  • Access Your Data: View all your information in the app settings
  • Export Your Data: Download your data in CSV or JSON format
  • Edit Information: Update or correct any personal or health data
  • Delete Your Account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Manage Permissions: Control camera, notification, and health app access
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and crash reporting in settings

5.2 Data Deletion

To delete your account and all data, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 90 days.

6. Children's Privacy

CalorieCompanion is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at the email below, and we will delete such information.

7. Health Data Special Protections

We recognize that health and fitness data is sensitive personal information requiring special protection:

  • Health data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access to health data requires authentication
  • We comply with applicable health data regulations
  • Health data is never used for marketing purposes
  • You maintain full control over your health information

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in South Africa where Prickly Pair Studios is based. We ensure that any international transfer of your data is protected by appropriate safeguards, including encryption and secure data transfer protocols. We comply with applicable data protection laws regardless of where your data is processed.

9. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 2 years may be deleted with 30-day notice
  • After Deletion: Personal data deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Backups: Backup systems may retain data for up to 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

10. Third-Party Services

CalorieCompanion may integrate with these third-party services:

  • Apple Health: Optional integration for iOS users
  • Google Fit: Optional integration for Android users
  • Firebase: App performance monitoring and crash reporting
  • Food Databases: USDA and other nutrition databases for food information

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies if you choose to use these integrations.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email to your registered address
  • A prompt to review and accept the updated policy

Your continued use of CalorieCompanion after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Compliance with Regulations

CalorieCompanion complies with applicable data protection regulations:

  • POPIA (South Africa): Protection of Personal Information Act compliance
  • GDPR (European Union): General Data Protection Regulation for EU users
  • CCPA (California): California Consumer Privacy Act for California residents
  • COPPA (United States): Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
  • App Store Guidelines: Apple App Store privacy requirements
  • Google Play Policies: Google Play Store data safety requirements

13. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
