Current Project: Smelify

One of our most exciting projects is Smelify, currently in the ideation phase. Smelify is an advanced olfactory technology that integrates the sense of smell into digital experiences, transforming business engagement by adding a new sensory dimension. Potential applications include:

  • E-Commerce: Allowing consumers to smell products online, enhancing shopping experiences.
  • Gaming and Entertainment: Adding realistic scents to virtual environments, increasing immersion.
  • Healthcare: Utilizing aromatherapy and sensory therapy in medical settings.
